# Mask off (title)

Itch.io
Target: Family
Rating: E (Everyone)

## Game Summary

Do you know beat 'em ups? Time to beat each other up! Four players, three maps, many different masks.

## Game Outline

Each players starts with a basic form, waiting for the visible timer to run down, for the special Mask to spawn! Each Mask gives a different power-up and effect. Use them creatively or brute force your way through your opponents face!

## USP (Unique Selling Points)

- Up to 4 player local!
- Super fun and deluxe quirky power ups in form of diverce masks!
- Three (3) different levels to outplay your friends!
